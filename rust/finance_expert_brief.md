# Finance Expert Brief

## Executive Summary
The finance team must publish a preliminary close summary by 10:00 AM Eastern on the second business day after month end. Revenue variances greater than 8% against plan and operating expense overruns greater than $25,000 require escalation to the CFO or director approval respectively. The finance team maintains a rolling 13-week cash forecast updated every Friday with threshold notifications for minimum liquidity.

## Control Signals
- Any revenue variance greater than 8% against plan must be escalated to the CFO with a written explanation (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
- Vendor payments above $50,000 require dual approval from Finance and Operations (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
- The finance team maintains a rolling 13-week cash forecast updated every Friday, triggering notifications if forecasted cash drops below the minimum liquidity threshold of $1.5 million (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
- Board finance packets should summarize revenue performance, material cost drivers, cash position, liquidity risk, and any control exceptions (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
