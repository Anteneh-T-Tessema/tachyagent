## Executive Summary

Key highlights include preliminary close summaries by 10:00 AM Eastern on the second business day after month end, dual approval for vendor payments above $50,000, and notification of the CFO if forecasted cash drops below $1.5 million.

## Control Signals

- Vendor payments above $50,000 require dual approval from Finance and Operations (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
- If forecasted cash drops below the minimum liquidity threshold of $1.5 million, the controller must notify the CFO and pause discretionary spend reviews (finance_controller_brief.txt, chunk 1).
